# ISSUE-NNNN: short title

**Status:** open | resolved | closed (won't fix)
**Filed:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Filed during:** change-id (if applicable) | session
**Kind:** observation | tech-debt | feature | question | trigger | soft-warning

## What

One paragraph describing the issue concretely. Avoid abstractions; name the file, the function, the spec section, the actual symptom.

## Why it matters

Why this isn't just noise. What's the impact if we don't address it? Who notices?

## Context

Where it came from — link to the change or session that surfaced it. If it's a deferred cell from a manifest, name the cell key (e.g., `engine_search.observability` deferred during change 0007).

## Proposed action

What would resolve this. May be empty for `observation` and `question` kinds.

## Resolution (filled when closed)

What was done, where, and what change closed it. Date.
