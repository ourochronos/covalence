# Research Paper Tracking

Papers scored on 5 dimensions (1-5 each, 25 max):
- **R** (Relevance): How directly applicable to Covalence's domains
- **C** (Recency): 2026=5, 2025=4, 2024=3, 2023=2, older=1
- **A** (Actionability): Could we implement techniques from this paper?
- **G** (Gap): Does it fill a knowledge gap in our graph?
- **I** (Impact): Citation count, venue quality, novelty

Status: `[ ]` = not ingested, `[x]` = ingested, `[~]` = partially ingested
